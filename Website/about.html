What's the Scoop?

Scoop Ice Cream Trucks was founded in 2017. At it's core, Scoop was created to be a fun, quirky business that sold happiness. Happiness in a cone, that is. Scoop was spawned out of a lust for fun and the skills to make delicious ice cream. All our ice cream is handmade with local ingredients. At this point in time, we have 2 trucks and 4 carts to our disposal. Each one of those has already made thousands of smiles of joy!

Meet the Team!

Our passionate team works hard every day to bring you the best ice cream you'll have ever tasted. It's all handcrafted according to old recipes. It's hard work, but these people love what they do!

Mathy Lippens

Founder

There are two things Mathy loves more above anything all. Summer and ice cream. That's how Scoop started. Mathy is the CEO, designer, director of sales, developer, driver and spokesperson for Scoop! He basically does everything, it's almost as if this is completely made up! His passion for this project seeps through every facet of his work.

Liesbeth Cooke

Ice Cream Specialist

Liesbeth comes from an Ice Cream Dynasty. Ice Cream runs through her veins. She never eats it though, cause you can't look like that and still eat ice cream! Nevertheless, Liesbeth's great grandfather was scooping ice cream all the way back in 1904 and the classical art of ice cream remained in the family line. Liesbeth is the brain behind all the production of our various flavous. She still uses her family's recipe book.

Bas

Executive Taste Tester

This is Bas. Bas loves ice cream. He also loves hot dogs, spaghetti, fries, dog food, cat food, ketchup, cake, sausages, chicken, rice, soup, candy, donuts, bread and who knows what else. All our new flavours get taste-tested by Bas before we sell them in our trucks. If Bas doesn't finish his bowl, we'll know we still have some work to do. Disclaimer: no dogs were harmed in the making of our ice cream.
